About me

Hi, my name is Redha BOUZID, from France
I am a generalist computer graphics designer specializing in 3D environments. With a background in architecture, I have participated in a wide range of productions in architecture, retail design, video games and object design. I have a versatile profile, technician and artist, indeed, I worked on many projects which I took in charge both the design, management and realization of computer graphics and animations.
In my free time, I like to explore new tools and new technologies in order to improve my skills.
